101-101

Loacl Tax (VS, MC,AM)

1

N

For Visa, the Visa Code indicates if local tax is included in the amount charged for the goods or services purchased.

For Mastercard, the Tax Exempt Indicator Code indicates if the goods or services were exempted from taxes.

Allowed values for Visa are:

0 = No tax included

1 = State or provincial tax included

2 = Non-taxable transaction

Allowed values for Mastercard are:

Spaces = Unknown or not applicable

0 = No Tax Collected

1 = Tax Exempt

102-113

Local Tax Amount

12

N

For Visa, the amount of any state or provincial tax included in the transaction amount. The currency in which this amount is given must be the same as the amount of the source transaction.

This field has two implied decimal places.

The field must be right justified. You can use zeroes as filler. The field can have all zeroes.

For Mastercard and Discover, the amount of tax assessed on the transaction.

Format:
999999999999
